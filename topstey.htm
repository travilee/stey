Here's some info on STEY:

STEY meets weekly. Email us for more information on the exact time and location. Or call 785.232.3100 (ask for Travis).

Why don't I just tell you right here where we meet and when? A couple of reasons: one, mean people in our town sometimes find it necessary to harass glbt folks and we don't want them showing up with nasty little picket signs at our meetings; two, some nights we do things out in the community so it's a good idea to call ahead if it's your first time to attend to make sure where we're going to be; and three, you have to be within a certain age range to go to STEY. Right now that limit is set at 13-25 years-old.

STEY is here for you to be who you are, free from prejudice, judgment, or harassment.

STEY is a Topeka AIDS Project sponsored social/support group offering:

  • An alcohol and drug-free social environment;

  • Peer and professional support;

  • Education about glbt history, life and current events;

  • Confidentiality at all times;

  • Fun, community-building events;

  • Information about HIV and other STD prevention;

  • A great place to meet other youth;

  • Strong role models;

  • Opportunities for volunteering and community involvement.
