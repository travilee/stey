MARCH 2003

 

 

EQUAL JUSTICE COALITION: BE A HATE FIGHTER! MULTIPLE WAYS TO GET INVOLVED NOW!

BE ALL YOU CAN BE! VOLUNTEER TODAY FOR YOUR OWN RIGHTS! Topeka City Council elections are coming up next month (Tuesday, April 1, 2003) and we've got a lot of opposition running against our fair-minded candidates. You can help by volunteering to go around the different neighborhoods encouraging people to register to vote, to vote for our candidates, and to get people to put up yard signs and by helping with mailings. It's a lot fun and even more so if you can get some friends to do it with you. Contact me today if you are interested.

YOUTH COALITION: Youth Activism Right Here in Topeka

The leaders of the local gay/straight alliances (GSAs) have recently come together to form a new coalition for youth. It's your way to become more involved in the community and ensure that the voices of our generation are better heard. Meetings for March will be on the 11th and 25th, 4 pm, at the TAP offices. Let me know if you are interested in attending before you go please.

 STEY Meetings

We have exciting meetings lined up in the next couple of weeks.
